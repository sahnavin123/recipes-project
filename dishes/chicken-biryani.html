Chicken Biryani

Chicken biryani is a classic South Asian dish that is made with fragrant basmati rice, tender chicken, and a blend of aromatic spices.
To make chicken biryani, the chicken is first marinated in a mixture of yogurt and spices, which helps to tenderize the meat and infuse it with flavor.
The rice is also soaked in water for a while to soften it and reduce the cooking time.

Ingredients

  • Chicken (cut into pieces)
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger paste, Garlic paste
  • Red chili powder, Cumin powder, Coriander powder, Garam masala, Turmeric powder
  • Salt
  • Basmati rice
  • Water
  • Bay leaf
  • Cinnamon stick
  • Cooking oil or ghee
  • Onion (sliced), Tomatoes (chopped), Green chilies (chopped)

Steps

  1. Marinate the chicken with spices and yogurt for at least 1 hour.
  2. Soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes and then cook until 70% done.
  3. Cook the chicken in a mixture of onions, tomatoes, and spices until it is fully cooked.
  4. Layer the cooked rice and chicken in a large pot or dish.
  5. Sprinkle saffron or food coloring on top of the rice.
  6. Cover the pot or dish and cook over low heat for 20-30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together and the rice to fully cook.
  7. Garnish with fried onions, fresh cilantro, and mint leaves.
  8. Serve hot with raita or other side dishes.

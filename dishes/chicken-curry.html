Chicken Curry

Chicken curry is a popular dish that is made with chicken, a flavorful sauce, and various spices.
The dish typically involves cooking chicken pieces in a sauce made from onion, garlic, ginger, and various spices. The spices used can include cumin, coriander, turmeric, chili powder, and garam masala, among others.

Ingredients

  • Chicken (usually bone-in for more flavor)
  • Cooking oil or ghee
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Spices (such as cumin, coriander, turmeric, chili powder, garam masala, and cinnamon)
  • Salt
  • Tomato puree, diced tomatoes, or tomato sauce
  • Coconut milk or yogurt (optional)
  • Fresh cilantro (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Prepare the ingredients: Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces and chop the onion, garlic, and ginger.
  2. Brown the chicken: Heat oil or ghee in a pan and add the chicken pieces. Cook them until they are browned on all sides, then remove them from the pan and set aside.
  3. Cook the aromatics: In the same pan, add more oil if needed and saut√© the onion, garlic, and ginger until they are fragrant and softened.
  4. Add the spices: Add the spices to the pan and cook for a minute or two until they are fragrant and well-distributed.
  5. Add the liquid: Add the tomato puree, diced tomatoes, or tomato sauce to the pan and stir well. If using coconut milk or yogurt, add it now as well.
  6. Simmer the curry: Add the chicken back to the pan and stir well to coat it in the sauce. Bring the mixture to a simmer, then reduce the heat and cover the pan. Let the curry simmer for 20-30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and tender.
  7. Adjust the seasoning: Taste the curry and add salt or additional spices as needed.
  8. Serve the curry: Spoon the chicken curry into bowls and garnish with fresh cilantro. Serve with rice, naan bread, or roti.
